## Format Rules

### Advancement
- **Auto advance**: Top 2 from each group (24 teams)
- **Best 3rd**: 8 best third-placed teams across all 12 groups
- **Total**: 32 teams → Round of 32

### Tiebreakers (3rd place comparison)
1. Points
2. Goal difference
3. Goals scored
4. Fair play points (disciplinary)
5. Drawing of lots

### Round of 32 Bracket
- 24 seeded teams (group 1st/2nd) in fixed positions
- 8 third-place teams slotted via predetermined bracket map
- Ensures no groupmates meet again in Round of 32
